    Solar Car Success - Supported by Peterbilt Motors Co., the Texas A&M Solar Car Racing Team is racing into the future.

    The Texas A&M Solar Car Racing Team, with support from Peterbilt Motors Co., competed in the Electrek Formula Sun Grand Prix using their solar-powered car, Duodecim. Revived in 2022, the team is advancing clean transportation research while preparing future engineers for the industry.

    Racing Across America: Solar Team Takes 11th in National Competition

    The Texas A&M Solar Car Racing Team competed in the Formula Sun Grand Prix and the American Solar Challenge, finishing 11th overall and 7th in their division out of over 40 teams nationwide.

    One Student’s Sustainable Legacy: Removing ‘Forever Chemicals’ and Racing Solar Cars

    Mechanical engineering graduate student Ian Burress was honored by the Office of Sustainability for his research on removing ‘forever chemicals’ and his work reviving the solar racing car team.
