About Me

I am a mechanical engineer built for high-stakes environments. My technical philosophy wasn't just shaped in the lab—it was forged on the mats as an NCAA Division I wrestler and tested in the pit lane suppressing live engine fires as a track marshal.

I specialize in bridging the gap between high-fidelity hardware validation and aggressive performance criteria. Whether I am executing complex powertrain testing, designing structural chassis components, or managing cross-country racing logistics, I thrive in spaces where rigorous precision and extreme pressure intersect.

Applied Engineering

  • FPV Drone Systems: Applied mechatronics. I design, solder, and PID-tune high-speed cinematic quadcopters, balancing thrust-to-weight ratios with structural resilience.
  • Automotive Prototyping: I treat project vehicles as live testbeds, executing hands-on custom fabrication, telemetry monitoring, and mechanical performance tuning.
  • Motorsports Operations: Active trackside deployment, translating mechanical intuition into rapid-response safety interventions and technical compliance inspections.
